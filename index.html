What is Formula 1

Formula 1 is the premier motorsport racing series, featuring high-speed, open-wheel cars with cutting-edge technology, global races, and a focus on speed, strategy, and innovation.

F1 races feature a grid of 20 drivers competing on various tracks worldwide. Races involve high-speed duels, pit stops, and strategic maneuvering to secure victory.
